Myers-Briggs Test Results - Advocate (INFJ-A)

The results for this test identify me as the type that is able to identify the motives of others and defuse conflict effectively, and operates with strong ethics. The Advocate personality will occasionally need their own time as well due to being of an introverted nature, and are not comfortable working with people less ethically inclined.

Hermann Brain Dominance Instrument - 1121

I took part in this paid test as part of a leadership training program in a previous work role. The test defines four quadrants of your brain and the degree of which you use it. My result shows that I have a relatively balanced load between the quadrants, but slightly favour the Analytical quadrant, which focuses on facts, critical thinking, and logic.

Learning Style Test Results - Solitary Dominant

This test shows that I would likely be most comfortable in solitary study... Which I suppose is really, really lucky right now.


Conclusion

Taking these results with a grain of salt, I agree with most of the details that are presented. What I do take away from these are insights into how I may be perceived by others, so in that regard I think there is real value to be found. The results wouldn’t really change my behaviour in a team environment, I’m very comfortable working with new people and am used to the challenges and conflicts that can come with the scenario. When forming a team, I am happy to work with anyone that is willing to put in the effort to support the team accordingly.

With that said, I respect proactive team members who are willing to put in the time and effort to create something meaningful, and I'm always looking for a way to help them accomplish that.
