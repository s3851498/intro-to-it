My Ideal Job

Link to Seek: Software Developer

This position as a software developer is with a company that proclaims itself the leaders in custom built virtual and augmented reality applications, which sounds exactly like the kind of work I was interested in exploring. The idea of working with a company with goals that align with me own is a great position to have available.

The position itself is at a senior level, and would revolve around the process of developing, managing, and deploying effective applications. It looks to also act as a leadership position, which would be a position I hope to achieve on this path.

Six years industry experience is mandatory to be considered, as well as tertiary qualifications, and existing clean code to show. Unity and C# are the primary requests regarding tool/language proficiency at a minimum. Unsurprisingly, Git is an essential request too.

Currently, I am new to the industry, have yet to complete my degree, and am lacking in programming language knowledge. However, I do have talent in leadership and project management, and believe I can handle deployment better than most would for a position such as this. I think if I could work through six years in the industry, I would be in a good standing to take a position like this.

In order to make up the desirable traits for a role like this, I plan to spend the next three years devoting as much time as I can to mastering relevant subjects and getting myself into the industry as soon as possible. Even when I find myself working in the industry, I am very interested in developing my own portfolio of code of software. I intend to work on personal projects throughout the next decade to build a solid foundation of ability and reputation, and hopefully make a name for myself as a contributor towards the future of technology.
